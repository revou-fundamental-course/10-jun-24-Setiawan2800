How to Convert Celsius (°C) to Fahrenheit (°F)

To convert a temperature in Celsius to Fahrenheit, multiply the Celsius temperature by 9/5 and then add 32.

Formula: SF = (SC × 9/5) + 32

Or: SF = (SC × 1.8) + 32

To convert Fahrenheit to Celsius, subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit temperature and then multiply by 5/9.

Formula: SC = (SF - 32) × 5/9

Or: SC = (SF - 32) / 1.8
